# Video Resources

## Purpose

This file holds curated public video anchors for Executive Fast Track modules. Resources should come from reputable public sources such as executive education institutions, business schools, governance bodies, consulting firms, official conference channels, and recognised practitioner sources.

## Curation rules

- Prefer official source channels.
- Prefer concise, high-signal videos.
- Avoid generic motivational content.
- Avoid pirated course material.
- Do not reproduce copyrighted transcripts without permission.
- Each video should map to a module and executive outcome.
