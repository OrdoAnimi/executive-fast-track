# Roadmap

## Product direction

Executive Fast Track starts as a private executive learning cockpit. It is first built to improve real-world executive fluency, interview performance, advisory confidence, and boardroom translation. Only after it proves useful should it become a public course or commercial product.

## Phase 1 — Private capability system

Build the course for practical use by one serious learner. The goal is immediate behavioural improvement: stronger executive language, better interview responses, stronger consulting framing, and more controlled boardroom presence.

Deliverables:

- Product operating system.
- Level 100 learning path.
- Executive persona model.
- Copilot prompt.
- Initial boardroom simulation flow.

## Phase 2 — Internal academy product

Turn the private capability system into a structured Velocity Academy course. The goal is repeatability: modules, progress, resource library, cool notes, and exercises that another learner can follow without direct explanation.

Deliverables:

- Static course site.
- Level 100 and Level 200 modules.
- Curated video and reading library.
- Module templates.
- Resource pages.

## Phase 3 — Simulation product

Make the Copilot the main experience. The goal is practice, not passive consumption. Learners bring real products, proposals, interviews, transformations, or consulting situations and rehearse executive engagement.

Deliverables:

- Boardroom Simulator.
- Objection map generator.
- Executive pitch builder.
- Decision memo builder.
- Role-based pressure tests.

## Phase 4 — Commercial readiness

Package the product for private beta. The goal is to test whether other senior professionals find the same value.

Deliverables:

- Public positioning.
- Beta learner journey.
- Pricing hypothesis.
- Feedback capture.
- Testimonial framework.
- Course completion artefacts.

## Phase 5 — External credential pathway

Map the course to external executive education pathways without claiming equivalence. The goal is to help learners identify next-step formal training such as AICD, MBA, executive finance, strategy, governance, or board-readiness programs.

Deliverables:

- External course map.
- AICD pathway notes.
- Global executive education benchmark notes.
- Optional study plans.
